About HashCards

What

HashCards is an online flashcard solution, but free of excessive spam and paywalls. With HashCards you can create, share, and study flashcards intuitively - for free - without being bombarded by constant ads, popups, or subscription fees.

Why

Although there are plenty of other online flashcard services, they suffer from poor user interfaces or excessive spam, bloat, and paywalls. HashCards was created as a simple, easy to use, and full-featured online flashcard service, just without the spam, bloat, and paywalls.

When

HashCards is looking to finish a complete pre-release in August 2023

Who
